About Us

Founded in 2024, Hand & Spoon Ice Cream brings you the freshest homemade ice cream made with exclusive recipes and top-quality ingredients. With over 100 flavors, we're dedicated to creating delicious and creamy treats for everyone!

Our Menu

We make our ice cream fresh daily, offering a variety of flavors. Here are some of our favorites:

  • Chocolate Fudge Brownie - Rich and creamy with chunks of fudge brownie
  • Vanilla Bean Dream - Classic, smooth vanilla with real vanilla beans
  • Mint Chocolate Chip - Refreshing mint with crunchy chocolate chips
  • Caramel Crunch - Sweet caramel with a crunchy surprise
  • Seasonal: Pumpkin Spice - Perfect for the fall with a hint of spice

We also serve yogurt, shakes, and sorbets to suit everyone's preferences!
